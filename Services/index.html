Services

Casadora may work with customers who have a strong vision, but we are also honored to guide others through the design process and help them to formulate their own aesthetic as we go.

- INTERIORS -

Our design prospective is informed by respect for historical architecture coupled with an appreciation of classical design. Casadora believe in creating aesthetically exciting interiors, also providing a personal commitment to our customers in each aspect of interior design for a project; from space planning, budget definition and design concepts to final installation.

We strive for sumptuous, imaginative interiors, reflecting our clients’ aspirations, lifestyles, personalities and brand, and no two jobs are ever alike.

- BESPOKE FURNISHINGS -

We produce made to measure, fitted furniture, lovingly crafted and impeccably made, installed by our experienced craftsmen. Entirely produced in our own factories in all its components, Casadora is proud to offer the widest choice of materials to match your requirements and yet orderly combine them to achieve the accomplished style required by the clients.

Every details counts when a client works with Casadora: from floor planning to rendering, from moodboards of unique international brands to day-to-day conversations until completion.
